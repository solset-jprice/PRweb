Rosen Method is a gentle yet powerful modality that focuses on bringing physical and emotional awareness to muscles that are chronically tense. These chronically tense places, which may have once served as a protection, often becomes a barrier, a limitation to the full expression of our aliveness. Rosen Method helps the client feel more deeply into these tight places sometimes leading to the surfacing of a long forgotten memory or subconsciously stored emotion.

This work is holistic in the sense that it works with the whole person; emotions, physical body, mental thoughts and belief structures all make up who we are and greatly effect how we see and relate to the world around us.

Some people come to Rosen Method for relief from physical or emotional pain. Others come seeking personal growth and expansion. Come experience for yourself how Rosen Method bodywork supports the journey into the depths of who we are and the rediscovery of our lost potential.
